## 1. PURPOSE

This Code of Conduct establishes expectations for all individuals and organizations using, contributing to, or benefiting from the dyerjohnelwithoutrecourse-dev infrastructure, systems, and documentation.

### 2.3 Responsibility
- ✅ Take responsibility for actions
- ✅ Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
- ✅ Protect data and security

- ✅ Comply with data protection regulations

## 3. EXPECTED BEHAVIOR

### 3.1 Intellectual Property Respect
- ✅ Acknowledge and attribute original creators
- ✅ Do not claim others' work as your own
- ✅ Follow license terms and attribution requirements
- ✅ Maintain clear modification documentation
- ✅ Respect trademarks and patents

### 3.2 Data Protection and Privacy
- ✅ Handle personal data securely
- ✅ Comply with GDPR, CCPA, and local privacy laws
- ✅ Protect data subject rights
- ✅ Report breaches and incidents
- ✅ Implement appropriate security measures

### 3.3 Honest Communication
- ✅ Communicate truthfully and accurately
- ✅ Disclose conflicts of interest
- ✅ Share relevant information with stakeholders
- ✅ Correct misinformation promptly
- ✅ Avoid misleading or deceptive statements

### 3.4 Security and Compliance
- ✅ Implement appropriate security measures
- ✅ Maintain system and data integrity
- ✅ Report security vulnerabilities responsibly
- ✅ Cooperate with security assessments
- ✅ Follow security best practices

### 3.5 Professional Conduct
- ✅ Act professionally in all interactions
- ✅ Avoid harassment or discrimination
- ✅ Treat others with respect
- ✅ Maintain professional boundaries
- ✅ Report misconduct through proper channels

### 3.6 Legal Compliance
- ✅ Comply with applicable laws and regulations
- ✅ Comply with export controls and sanctions
- ✅ Comply with financial regulations
- ✅ Comply with industry standards
- ✅ Report legal violations

## 4. PROHIBITED CONDUCT

### 4.1 Intellectual Property Violations
- ❌ Claiming others' work as your own
- ❌ Removing or obscuring attribution
- ❌ Misrepresenting authorship or origin
- ❌ Infringing patents or trademarks
- ❌ Unauthorized licensing of others' IP

### 4.2 Data and Privacy Violations
- ❌ Unauthorized access to personal data
- ❌ Sharing personal data without consent
- ❌ Failing to protect personal data
- ❌ Violating data subject rights
- ❌ Non-compliance with data protection laws

### 4.3 Security Violations
- ❌ Unauthorized system access
- ❌ Malware or virus distribution
- ❌ Denial of service attacks
- ❌ Security vulnerability exploitation
- ❌ System or data destruction

### 4.4 Fraudulent or Deceptive Conduct
- ❌ False representations or statements
- ❌ Misrepresentation of credentials
- ❌ Unauthorized impersonation
- ❌ Fraudulent financial transactions
- ❌ Conspiracy to defraud

### 4.5 Harassment or Discrimination
- ❌ Harassment based on protected characteristics
- ❌ Discriminatory treatment
- ❌ Bullying or intimidation
- ❌ Threats or violence
- ❌ Creating hostile environment

### 4.6 Unlawful Activity
- ❌ Violation of laws or regulations
- ❌ Criminal activity
- ❌ Export control violations
- ❌ Money laundering or sanctions evasion
- ❌ Tax evasion or fraud

## 5. ENFORCEMENT

### 5.1 Violation Reporting
Report violations through:
- Email: compliance@[domain]
- Issue tracker: [GitHub issues]
- Anonymous hotline: [Hotline number]
- Legal department: [Legal contact]

### 5.2 Investigation Process
- Confidential review of allegations
- Opportunity for respondent to respond
- Objective investigation
- Documentation of findings
- Communication of outcome

### 5.3 Consequences

**Warning** - For minor first violations:
- Written warning
- Required training or remediation
- Monitoring period

**Suspension** - For significant or repeated violations:
- Temporary access restriction
- Required compliance activities
- Probationary period

**Termination** - For severe or persistent violations:
- Permanent access removal
- Legal action if appropriate
- Reporting to authorities if required

**Legal Action** - For criminal violations:
- Reporting to law enforcement
- Cooperation with investigations
- Civil or criminal litigation

### 5.4 Conflict Resolution
- Good faith resolution attempts
- Mediation services available
- Escalation procedures
- Appeal processes
